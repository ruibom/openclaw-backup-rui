📉 Cultural Fit is just a polite term for hiring people who look and think exactly like you. I call this "Operational Stagnation."

When you hire for "Fit," you are essentially buying more of what you already have. You are amplifying your existing blind spots.

If your team is 10 "Hustlers" with zero "Systems" people, hiring an 11th hustler doesn't make you faster. It just makes your crashes louder.

Real scalers doesn't hire for "Fit." They hire for Culture Add.

They look for the missing piece of the "Operational Puzzle." They identify the specific friction point in their current output and hire the person who naturally reduces that friction.

If your inputs are "Same + Same," your outputs will remain "Same."

Top-tier companies like Stripe and Netflix don't want people who "fit in." They want people who "challenge the baseline" while adhering to the core mission.

Stop hiring for vibes. Start hiring for additive friction-reduction.

Are you building a family, or an elite high-output machine?
