2. Interval Party – 4×250m descending pace + underwater kicks
3. Breathwork Builder – Breathe every 5/7/9 strokes + hypoxic sprints
4. Open-Water Sim – Zig-zag sighting + pull buoy
5. Fun Finisher – Last 200m IM or with fins
