### Q3: How do you approach forecasting and revenue analytics?

Here's an uncomfortable truth: most forecasts are really just opinions dressed up in data clothing.

My approach is different. I use what I call the **Commit-Expected-Best** model:

- **COMMIT**: What the rep is 100% confident will close - signed contract, verbal agreement, the works
- **EXPECTED**: Weighted probability based on deal stage, rep history, and deal health
- **BEST**: The upside scenario - competitive win, unexpected expansion, lucky break

I build this directly into HubSpot with custom fields and roll-ups. Every week, everyone knows exactly what the number is. Not "somewhere between $50,000 and $500,000." A specific number, with specific confidence.

At Penbrothers, this forecast model was a key driver of the 3x revenue growth. When leadership knows what to expect, they can plan. Investors can trust. The team can focus on what actually moves the needle.

I also use advanced Excel and Google Sheets for scenario modeling - capacity planning, territory analysis, quota allocation. But the key principle is always the same: **the model should tell you what to do, not just what happened.**

## Automation Ideas I'd Bring

Here's what I'd tackle in my first 30 days - the quick wins that demonstrate immediate value:

### 1. Multi-Region Lead Routing Engine

**The Problem:** Managing leads across different regions (US, Europe, APAC) with different hotel markets, booking patterns, and sales cycles. Currently, there's no unified routing logic - leads slip through cracks, response times vary wildly by region.

**The Solution:** A HubSpot workflow that automatically routes leads based on geography, hotel size, and region-specific buying patterns - with timezone-aware follow-up sequences that match each region's business hours.

**Time to Build:** 2-4 hours
**Expected ROI:** 20-30% improvement in response time, 15% lift in conversion rates

---

### 2. CRM Hygiene Automation Suite

**The Problem:** A 35-person RevOps organization generates massive data decay. Duplicates, incomplete records, abandoned fields - all of it adds up to garbage forecasts and wasted sales cycles.

**The Solution:** Automated duplicate detection and merge workflows. A weekly data quality scorecard that tracks completeness, accuracy, and recency. Auto-fill for missing firmographic data using hotel industry data providers.

**Time to Build:** 3-5 hours
**Expected ROI:** Cleaner data = reliable forecasts = trust = better decisions

---

### 3. Cross-Region Forecasting Dashboard

**The Problem:** Different regions have different fiscal calendars, currencies, and sales cycles. Getting a unified view of pipeline health across all regions is currently manual and error-prone.

**The Solution:** A unified dashboard that rolls up all deals from all regions into one view. Segments by region with one-click filtering. Shows Commit-Expected-Best for each region and total. Projects end-of-quarter forecasts based on current velocity.

**Time to Build:** 4-6 hours
**Expected ROI:** Real-time visibility into pipeline across all regions, no more manual spreadsheet combining
