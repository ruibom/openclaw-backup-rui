## Job Application Process (Required)

For EVERY job application, follow this extra step:

### Step 1: Company GTM Analysis
Research the company to understand:
- What they sell, who they target
- Their current stage (Series A/B/C, growth)
- Current GTM challenges (search for "challenges", "problems", "pain" on their site/blog)
- Tech stack (CRM, tools mentioned in job description)

### Step 2: Automation Ideas (2-3)
For each application, brainstorm 2-3 simple automations (buildable in a day) that could:
- Speed up their workflows
- Reduce manual work
- Improve data quality/visibility

Format: One-pager or bullet list to include with application

### Step 3: Include with Application
Attach or embed the automation ideas to show proactive value-add

### Step 4: Track in Applications
- Add to [applications-tracker.md](./applications-tracker.md)
- Include: company, role, URL, status, submitted date, response
